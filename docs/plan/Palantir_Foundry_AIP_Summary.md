# Palantir Foundry & AIP – Executive Summary

## Overview
Palantir was founded in 2003 by Peter Thiel and former PayPal colleagues. The name comes from the *Palantiri* in Lord of the Rings, symbolizing the ability to make hidden information visible. Palantir initially focused on intelligence and defense data integration challenges that emerged after 9/11.

## Origins and Gotham
- In-Q-Tel, the CIA-backed venture fund, invested in Palantir in 2004.
- Palantir launched **Gotham** in 2008.
- Gotham connects intelligence data sources such as:
  - Field reports
  - Signals intelligence
  - Surveillance systems
  - Operational databases
  - Location and event data
- It helps analysts discover relationships between people, places, events, and activities.

## The Core Problem Palantir Solves
Organizations often have critical information distributed across disconnected systems.

Palantir's approach is:
1. Connect existing systems.
2. Integrate siloed data.
3. Create a unified operational view.
4. Enable better analysis and decision-making.

## Foundry – Enterprise Platform
After success in government and defense, Palantir expanded into commercial markets through **Foundry**.

Foundry integrates:
- Manufacturing data
- Financial systems
- Procurement systems
- Logistics information
- Supply-chain data
- Operational workflows

The platform allows organizations to build:
- Dashboards
- Operational applications
- Decision-support systems
- Workflow automation

## Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs)
One of Palantir's unique strengths is its **Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs)**.

FDEs:
- Work directly with customers
- Embed within client organizations
- Integrate systems
- Understand business processes
- Build operational applications
- Drive measurable business outcomes

They serve as the bridge between technology and business operations.

## Ontology
Ontology is a semantic layer built on top of enterprise data.

Instead of viewing data as tables and records, Ontology models real-world entities such as:
- Suppliers
- Factories
- Products
- Shipments
- Payments
- Contracts
- Risks

This allows users and AI systems to reason using business concepts instead of database structures.

## AIP – Artificial Intelligence Platform
Following the rise of ChatGPT and Large Language Models, Palantir launched **AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)**.

AIP combines:
- AI Models
- Gotham
- Foundry
- Ontology

Capabilities include:
- Natural language interaction
- Workflow automation
- Recommendations
- Enterprise decision support
- Governed AI operations

## Why AI Works Well in Palantir
Many AI projects fail because enterprise data is fragmented.

Palantir solves this problem first by:
1. Integrating data.
2. Establishing governance.
3. Creating contextual relationships.
4. Providing a trusted operational foundation.

As a result, AI can operate on reliable, connected enterprise information.
